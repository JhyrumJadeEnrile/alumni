Bahay ng Alumni — Architectural Render

🏛 Bahay ng Alumni

A dedicated alumni center on campus — a permanent space for alumni gatherings, meetings, mentorship, and memories.

  • Multi-story facility with modern architecture designed for multipurpose use.
  • Designed to serve as the permanent home of the MTI Alumni Association.
  • Will house meeting rooms, event spaces, a legacy exhibit, and alumni services.
  • A living monument to every graduate who walked through TUP-Taguig's gates.

Become a Business Partner

Your alma mater. Your market partner. The TUP MTI Alumni Association connects alumni entrepreneurs with a powerful network of fellow graduates, supporters, and the wider TUP-Taguig community.

📣

Market Reach

Get your brand in front of 2,500+ TUP-T alumni and their networks. List your business in our directory and be discovered by fellow graduates who prefer to support one of their own.

🤝

Alumni Network

Connect directly with other alumni entrepreneurs for potential B2B partnerships, referrals, and collaborations. Our community thrives when we do business with each other.

🏷️

Exclusive Visibility

Feature your products and services at alumni events, homecomings, and sportsfests. Be part of pop-up markets, trade fairs, and sponsored activities organized by the association.

📈

Shared Legacy

As a TUP-T graduate and business owner, your success is part of our shared legacy. Bring your brand home — let's leverage the power of our community to expand your market reach.

Ready to List Your Business?

Getting listed is simple. Join our official alumni community group, post your business details, and our team will help feature you in the directory. It's free for verified alumni members.
